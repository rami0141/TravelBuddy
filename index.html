TravelBuddy is an app that helps a traveler plan trips. After entering a location and a date range, TravelBuddy presents the user with a map, a weather display and a selection of activity categories to choose from. When the user chooses a category, the map populates with markers at locations in that category. The user can click on the markers for information and then may add those places to lists that he can refer to later.
